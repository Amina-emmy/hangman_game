hangman Game Guide

Two friends sit together, one is the word chooser and the other is the guesser.

The word chooser : types a word into the input field and clicks the "Go" button. The length of the word is then displayed.

The guesser : starts with 7 attempts, he clicks on the letter buttons to guess the word. If the guessed letter is correct, it fills in the corresponding blank spaces in the word. If it's not, the remaining attempts decreases.

Enjoy the challenge of guessing words and avoiding the hangman!
